üåø About Zero Waste Lucknow

Zero Waste Lucknow is an initiative by Nagar Nigam Lucknow to promote sustainable waste management and create a cleaner, greener Lucknow. Our mission is to transform the way citizens think about waste by turning it into a valuable resource.

Through our innovative app, residents can easily sell their recyclable waste and earn money while contributing to environmental conservation. We collect various types of recyclables including paper, plastic, metal, glass, and e-waste from your doorstep.
